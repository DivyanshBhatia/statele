How to play ›
Round 1: guess the highlighted state or union territory from its shape in 6 tries — each guess shows distance, direction and proximity to the answer. Then a series of bonus rounds tests what you know about that same state: its capital and where it sits, locating the state on the map of India, how many states border it and naming those neighbours, a major river, then spotting a famous landmark by its photo, naming it and locating it on the map, the main languages, its largest district, and its area. Earn a badge for each one you get right. Stuck on a round? Use See answer & skip to move on, or Give up & see results to finish early — then share your run.
